About Me

Hello! I don't want my personal info online, but you can call me Smasho! I've enjoyed creating things for as long as I can remember—from drawing on pieces of paper to making levels in level editors to creating Discord bots to, now, my own website!

I picked this name because I was in love with Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, so I added an "o" at the end of "smash" because it sounded nice.

I inadvertantly picked up the hamster theme in the latter half of 2022 when I thought Chomik was cute and made him my profile picture. Soon after, I was always associated with hamsters, so I have since embraced it and turned into a real life hamster. The profile picture you see above is a hamster, not a cat.
